About me

I am the Data Science and Machine Learning Team Lead for Opto Biosystems based in Cambridge. Previously, I was a Sir Henry Wellcome Research Fellow in computational neuroscience at the University of Cambridge where I worked with Máté Lengyel, Mark Stokes, John Duncan, and several other collaborators. Before that, I did my PhD at the University of Oxford in computational neuroscience under the supervision of Tim Vogels, Mason Porter, and Guillaume Hennequin.
